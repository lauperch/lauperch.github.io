Homerun

New in town? Want to explore the place you live in?

Check out homerun!

You may choose any point on the map as your destination.

You will then be dropped in a 2km radius around this point.

The goal is to run home as fast as possible in Google Street View.

Good luck, have fun

Flaggr

//Comming soon(-ish)

The world needs yet another social network

Flaggr lets you drop flags on the maps at the point you are right now..

But you may see flags from all over the worlds.

Flags consist of three parts (at least one is requred): Text, Image, Video

The idea is that only local people can drop flags at their home.

This gives better a insight on what's going on in the world.

Enjoy

Leechd

Did someone ever steal your phone? Your bike? Your laptop?

Leechd is a small script that polls ricardo.ch to find it!

If a candidate is found, you will be notified.

For now, it only works on Ricardo, but it can be extended in the future.

Good luck finding your stuff =)
